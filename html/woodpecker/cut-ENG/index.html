Our Mission at Woodpecker Technologies is to revolutionize verification and validation of IC’s and systems through a complete paradigm shift. Our technology is automated and comprehensive vs today’s manual and guesswork-based methodologies.

With Woodpecker's Lockstep, design houses can then purely focus on design features and performance, developing better products. At the same time, they can cut months of time-to-market from both the pre-silicon and post-silicon stages.

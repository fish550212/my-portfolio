Company Mission
Company Mission

Our vision is to provide disruptive end-to-end verification solutions across all elements of IC design.

We are pioneering a new era where design houses can focus on features and performance and rely on Woodpecker for a completely automated verification solution.
We are called “Woodpecker” because we can look deep into the design to find the biggest and juiciest bugs!

Products and Engagements

Woodpecker has an entire line of automated verification products planned. Currently 3 of these products have been developed and are servicing Tier 1 paying customers, Alibaba and MediaTek.

Technology

For pre-silicon verification, we offer a comprehensive, formal analysis-based, full-SOC verification. We are currently deployed on multiple commercial RISC-V designs and finding bugs not possible using traditional random-based simulation methodologies. Not only do we increase coverage, but we can produce bug traces of around 10-20 instructions, much shorter than simulation and easier for designers to debug.

For post-silicon validation, we have developed our own compiler to take existing benchmarks and System-Level-Tests and generate enhanced tests that increase bug detection 4x while decreasing Error Detection Latency over 1,000,000x, from many seconds down to microseconds.

For the verification IP, our technology enables design houses to localize bugs down to a certain flip flop area, so the validation engineer knows exactly where the bug has occurred. This innovative product is the first one to use formal analysis in a post-silicon setting.

Technology Support

Woodpecker has significant industry support due to its ground-breaking research and innovation. In 2019 during the academic stage, this technology was already identified by the IEEE roadmap.

In TextConX China 2021, keynote Speaker Harry Chen described highlighted our post-silicon products and even described one as “DFT for systems”

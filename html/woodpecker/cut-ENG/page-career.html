C++ Software Engineer Apply Now

Location
Shanghai, Hangzhou, Taiwan is preferred
Job Description
We are seeking a highly-motivated and detail-oriented senior software engineer with expertise in compilers to join our company. The role will be focused on verifying products that are based on innovative verification techniques that came from years of research at Stanford University and we are expanding and moving them into an industrial use. You will be responsible for building and maintaining a compiler (on top of LLVM and GCC) that is used to generate tests for HW verification and validation. This is a great opportunity to join an elite team and learn, contribute and innovate in an area that is so in need of a big improvement. The individual will also work with customers to integrate the tool in their verification flow.
Primary responsibilities

The responsibilities will be tailored to the candidate's skills and expertise and will include several of the following, but not be limited to:

  • Modifying LLVM and/or GCC and building a compiler with a particular kind of output.
  • Writing testplans and maintaining them.
  • Creating the verification environment for testing the new compiler for different ISAs such as RISC-V, ARM, etc.
  • Innovating new features and techniques for our verification solutions.
Minimum Qualifications
  • BS, MS, or PhD in EE/CS or equivalent degree.
  • 2+ (with BS) or 1+ (with MS/PhD) years of experience in SW engineering and verification.
  • Expert Linux user with experience in at least one programming language like C, C++ or Java and one scripting language for setting up verification process automation including regressions.
  • Good knowledge and experience with SW verification tools and concepts.
  • Experience with revision control methodology/systems such as Perforce or GIT.
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
  • Ability to work independently and at various levels of abstraction.
  • Strong communication, inter-personal and teamwork skills.
  • Strong desire to learn various skills needed in a start-up environment.
  • Ability to work effectively with both internal and external teams/customers.
Preferred Skills
The ideal candidate will be able to demonstrate the following behaviors:
  • Some experience in post-Si validation/debug.
  • Experience with using cloud computing platforms such as AWS or GCP.

RTL Design Engineer Apply Now

Location
Shanghai, Hangzhou, Taiwan is preferred
Job Description
We are seeking a highly-motivated and detail-oriented RTL design and verification engineer to join our company. The role will be focused on implementing and verifying products that are based on innovative verification techniques that came from years of research at Stanford University and we are expanding and moving them into an industrial use. You will be responsible for developing, debugging, and maintaining code for Woodpecker’s verification IPs, and testing it in formal verification environments that the verification IPs would be used in. This is a great opportunity to join an elite team and learn, contribute and innovate in an area that is in need of a big improvement. The individual will also work with customers to integrate the IPs in their verification flow.

Woodpecker offers an attractive and competitive compensation with meaningful equity for the selected candidate.
Primary responsibilities
The responsibilities will be tailored to the candidate's skills and expertise and will include several of the following, but not be limited to:
  • Developing deep understanding of Woodpecker’s IPs and products.
  • Innovating new features and techniques for our verification solutions.
  • Writing specification documents and maintaining them.
  • Working with verification engineers on testplans.
  • Writing RTL and PSL or SVA code.
  • Ensuring the logic design meets the architectural specifications.
  • Setting up RTL design environment, tools, methodologies, and infrastructure.
  • Analyzing verification coverage and debugging failures.
  • Expanding our IPs’ features list by analyzing micro-architectural features of target designs.
Qualifications
  • BS, MS, or PhD in EE/CS or equivalent degree.
  • 2+ (with BS) or 1+ (with MS/PhD) years of experience in verification of ASICs or IP blocks or SoCs.
  • Strong background in computer architecture and advanced knowledge of CPU.
  • Strong experience in parameterized and configurable Verilog/ System Verilog coding.
  • Strong experience with chip front-end design process including related 3rd-party RTL verification tools and revision control methodology/systems such as Perforce or GIT.
  • Proficient Linux user with experience in at least one programming language like C, C++ or Java and one scripting language for setting up a front-end design infrastructure and build environment.
  • Demonstrable knowledge and experience with formal model checking tools and concepts.
  • Familiarity with using cloud computing platforms such as AWS or GCP.
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
  • Ability to work independently and at various levels of abstraction.
  • Strong communication, inter-personal and teamwork skills.
  • Strong desire to learn various skills needed in a start-up environment.
  • Ability to work effectively with both internal and external teams/customers.
Preferred Skills
  • Experience with SoC architecture, integration and/or verification.
  • Familiarity with IP encryption methodologies and implementation.
  • Experience with on-chip bus protocols like ACE, AHB, AXI etc.
  • Knowledge of DFT (design for test), DFD (design for debug), and manufacturing test flow.
  • Experience in post-Si validation/debug.
  • Basic understanding of high-level language compiler technology.
